What is Hack Club?

Hack Club is a community of high schoolers who are interested in computer science, and is open to anyone from the most experienced coding veterans to those who are writing their first line of code today. Hack Club members learn by doing, making apps, websites, and games to gain programming experience, working with industry-level tools on a daily basis.

Agoura Hack Club is supported by the Hack Club organization, a network that encompasses hundreds of clubs and thousands of students just like us around the country and the world.
